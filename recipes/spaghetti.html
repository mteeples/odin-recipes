Spaghetti

Description

Cooking is really easy. I don't see why people make such a big deal about it. All you do is follow the instructions on the papckages of the ingredients. Maybe the best chefs are the best at reading instructions on packages of food.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1/2 onion, chopped
  • Pinch of garlic
  • 1 packet whole wheat pasta
  • 1 can tomato sauce

Steps

  1. Boil a whole bunch of water in a big pot.
  2. Follow the directions on the bag of pasta.
  3. Cook the ground beef with the onions and garlic.
  4. Add the tomato sauce.
  5. Serve with a salad and some bread.
