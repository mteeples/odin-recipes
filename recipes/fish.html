Fish

Description

Fish live good lives. They swim, swim, and swim some more. Then, they bring joy to the people that catch and cook them. They are delicious with rice and lemon.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup rice
  • A sprig of cilantro
  • A pinch of salt
  • 2 cups of water
  • 1 half of a lime
  • Some fish

Steps

  1. Place the rice and water into a pot. Bring the water to a boil.
  2. Reduce the heat to low, wait for the bubbles to lower in the pot, and cover the pot tightly with a lid.
  3. Let the rice sit on low heat for 15 minutes.
  4. Turn off the heat. Serve the rice with the fresh cilantro, lime, and salt
  5. Then add fish to taste.
